Our Story

Founded by a family of pizza lovers, Pizza Paradise started as a dream to bring authentic, delicious pizza to our community.
With over 20 years of experience in the culinary arts, our head chef has perfected a variety of recipes that reflect our passion for quality and flavor.
We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, ensuring that every pizza is fresh and full of flavor.
Our mission is to create a warm, welcoming environment where friends and family can gather to enjoy great food and great company
